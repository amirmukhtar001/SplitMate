Account Deletion - Smart Udhaar

Last updated: 2026-04-25

You can request account and data deletion from inside the app at Legal & Support → Request Account Deletion, or by email.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to amirmukhtar001@gmail.com.
  2. Use the same email address that you used to sign in to Smart Udhaar.
  3. Use subject: Account Deletion Request - Smart Udhaar.
  4. Include confirmation that you want permanent account deletion.

What data is deleted

  • Account profile linked to your sign-in email.
  • Loan records including borrower names, amounts, notes, reminders, and payment history stored for your account.

What may be retained

  • Minimal records may be retained if required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution.

Timeline

  • Deletion requests are acknowledged within 72 hours.
  • Requests are typically completed within 7-14 days after verification.
